Life Makeover Outfits

Created production-ready character outfits for Life Makeover, including full ownership of the Islet Fragrance and Night Roaming sets, along with contributions to additional costume pieces. The production pipeline included garment construction in Marvelous Designer, retopology in Maya, sculpted refinements in ZBrush, and material creation using Photoshop and Substance Designer.

Particular attention was given to intricate embroidery, lace, and stitching while maintaining efficient real-time performance. Reusable embroidery materials were developed to streamline production across multiple outfits. Each costume was adapted for both adult and child character models while preserving consistent proportions and visual quality.
